I contribute **Thai localization and translation** to open-source projects that
advance privacy, security, and an open internet, including:

- [**Mozilla Firefox**](https://www.mozilla.org/firefox/) — the open-source web browser.
- [**OONI Probe**](https://ooni.org/install/) — the app for measuring internet censorship.
- [**OONI Explorer**](https://explorer.ooni.org/) — the open dataset and dashboard of network-interference measurements.
- [**Signal**](https://signal.org/) — the private messaging app.

This work makes privacy and anti-censorship tools accessible to Thai-speaking
users, complementing my digital-rights advocacy with the
[Thai Netizen Network](https://thainetizen.org/).
