An **AI Bill of Materials** documents the components, datasets, and models that
make up an AI system, so that they can be audited for transparency,
accountability, and supply-chain security.

I co-wrote the Linux Foundation whitepaper
[*Building an AI Bill of Materials*](https://www.linuxfoundation.org/research/ai-bom)
and contribute to the **AI and Dataset profiles** of the
[System Package Data Exchange (SPDX)](https://github.com/spdx/spdx-spec)
specification — the open standard used to describe software (and now AI) bills
of materials.
