The [Data Privacy Vocabularies and Controls Community Group
(DPVCG)](https://w3c.github.io/dpv/) at the W3C develops the **Data Privacy
Vocabulary (DPV)** — a set of machine-readable terms for describing the
processing of personal data, legal bases, rights, risks, and, increasingly, AI.

I contribute to the vocabulary and its AI-related extensions, connecting
data-protection concepts to the broader work on AI accountability and
documentation standards.
