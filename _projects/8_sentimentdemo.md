[**sentimentdemo**](https://github.com/bact/sentimentdemo) is a deliberately
simple sentiment-analysis application published **solely as an artifact to
demonstrate an AI Bill of Materials** — how a real (if tiny) AI application can
ship with a complete software/AI BOM describing its models, datasets, and
dependencies.

It is not meant for serious text classification; it is a teaching example for
accountable AI documentation.
