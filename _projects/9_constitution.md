[**constitution**](https://github.com/bact/constitution) converts the Thai
constitution from PDF into clean, usable **plaintext**, correcting the Thai
character-encoding glitches that typically appear when extracting Thai text from
PDFs.

It is a small but practical open-data project at the intersection of Thai NLP
and civic technology.
